The Skill: Beyond implementation, I document complex technical systems. This project required implementing the Eisert-Wilkens-Lewenstein (EWL) protocol in Python and producing the formal academic analysis of the equilibrium shifts.

From the Abstract

This research paper investigates the implications of extending beyond the classical constraints that bound the players within the dilemma through the application of the Eisert-Wilkens-Lewenstein (EWL) protocol. Specifically, we analyze the emergence of a 'Quantum' strategy (Q) that utilizes the phase space of the qubit to counteract classical defection.
